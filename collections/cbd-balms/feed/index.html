How Our CBD Balm Works

To understand the benefits of CBD balm products, it’s important to understand their main ingredient: cannabidiol (CBD). CBD is one of a group of compounds found in the cannabis plant, called cannabinoids. There are over 100 different cannabinoids found in cannabis, and CBD is one of the two most prevalent (along with THC). 

When CBD and other phytocannabinoids (plant-based cannabinoids) are consumed, they interact with the body’s endocannabinoid system, mimicking the behavior of the body’s own endocannabinoids. Endocannabinoids interact with systems and organs throughout the body, helping them to maintain homeostasis, or balance. This interaction helps your body regulate a wide range of important functions, including pain, inflammation, stress, sleep, mood, memory, appetite, motor control, and much more. When CBD and other cannabinoids interact with receptors in the endocannabinoid system, they bolster the body’s ability to regulate these functions, resulting in a bevy of wellness benefits.

Topical CBD products are particularly effective in their interaction with CB2 receptors, which balance pain and inflammation. These benefits make CBD balm, CBD cream, and CBD lotion products so popular.

How to Use CBD Balm

Using a CBD balm is easy. Just rub the CBD balm stick on the affected area and let the CBD and other ingredients work their magic.
